About the Abhinav Kala Mahavidyalaya

Abhinav Kala Mahavidyalaya is a private institute, established in 2010 in Pune, Maharashtra. Abhinav Kala Mahavidyalaya offers a total of 1 course at UG levels. Furthermore, it offers courses such as BFA in Full Time mode. Abhinav Kala Mahavidyalaya has an annual seat intake of 30 for all the programmes.

It offers courses in stream such as Arts ( Fine / Visual / Performing ). The institute has a safe and well-equipped campus with all the advanced facilities.
