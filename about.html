About Us

Abhinav Kala Mahavidyalaya

Abhinav Kala Mahavidyalaya is a private institute, established in 2010 in Pune, Maharashtra. It offers BFA programs in Fine, Visual and Performing Arts.

The institute provides a creative learning environment with modern facilities and a strong focus on artistic development.

With a limited intake and focused curriculum, it ensures quality education and personal growth.
